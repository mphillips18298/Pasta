Homemade Spaghetti
  • Ingredients
  • Dough
  • Rolling
  • Cooking
  • Grocery List
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 pinch of kosher salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 (more) egg yolks
  • 2/3 tablespoons EVOO
  • note: this recipe requires the use of a pasta maker
Dough Prep
Step One

On a clean surface pour out flour and make a large well in the centre

Step Two

Scamble the eggs and oil together and pour into the flour well

Step Three

Start mixing the egg and flour slowly

Step Four

Once it is too hard to mix with the fork pick it up with your hands to start kneading

Step Five

Knead the dough for about 10 minutes

Step Six

Make it into a little bug monster-optional

Step Seven

Press your finger into the dough, if it bounces back wrap it up if it doesn't keep kneading

Previous Next
Rest

Put the wrapped dough in the fridge and let it rest for 30 mins

Rolling the Dough

Cut the dough

Cut the dough into 6-8 pieces and squish them until they are flat enough to go through the pasta roller

Roll

On the thickest setting (usually 6) roll the dough through, multiple time gradually reducing the setting number until the dough is thin enough that you can see your hand through it

Yay Spaghetti

Finally roll the pasta dough through the spaghetti attachment

Dry the noodles

Hang the noodle on something and flour them lightly so they don't stick and let them dry for about 15 mins.

Cooking

Boil Water

Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil, add the noodles to the water and cook for 2-3 mins.

ENJOY!

strain the noodles, and serve with chosen sauce! I've made a pesto sauce to enjoy with my fresh noodles!
